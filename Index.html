Collabora Academy

Work experience done right

May brought us Xavier Watts, a local student with a passion for motorcross, who came to us looking to get stuck into marketing and content production. Xavier spent the week working closely with Tabby and Angus in the content team, rounding things off with a marketing masterclass from Eloise. He brought genuine energy and a fresh perspective that reminded us why this matters. Real curiosity is contagious.

The week after, we had Alex Van-Zuylen join the marketing team. Alex threw himself into it, planning posts, exploring new spaces, and getting properly stuck into life at a creative agency. What he showed us is something we already believe: the right attitude opens everything. Students like Alex don't just gain experience here, they add to it.

Both arrived with open minds and easy-going natures, and both left having made a real impression.

Collabora Academy Placements

Last year's 20-week placements set a high bar. The students who came through didn't just observe. They got involved, contributed, and left a mark on the work.

That continued when we invited students back in to tackle something a little different: taking one of our previous projects and reimagining it from scratch, with a new set of rules. The results were genuinely impressive. Fresh thinking, bold ideas, and the kind of energy that makes a room sit up. Some of the work they produced is below and it speaks for itself.

Off the back of that, and the stand-out performances during the Project Failure presentations, we're moving forward with interviews for voluntary placements this week. Watch this space. Some new faces could be joining us soon.
